This inventory catalogues every page-image in the supplied PDF. "Voynichese" is the undeciphered native script; only ordinary Latin-alphabet text (library stamps, folio numbers, the modern pencil note, faded medieval marginalia) is transcribed. Colours noted are those actually present (green, blue, red-brown, ochre washes). Faint folio numbers in brown ink appear at top-right of most leaves.
